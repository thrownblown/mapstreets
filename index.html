Show Streets

  • Instructions for use:

  • Draw a polygon that represents your client's delivery zone by clicking at least three times on the map to the right.
  • Cick the "Show Streets" link above to return all of the streets inside the zone and the range of valid addresses.
  • It takes approx 30-40 seconds to run and it will notify you when its complete.
